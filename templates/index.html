About

MindReads assists in achieving SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being

Reading has been shown to have a number of positive effects on mental health, including reducing stress, improving cognitive function, and increasing empathy. Through book recommendations that address psychological needs and assist readers in taking control of their mental health, MindReads has the potential to support the promotion of good mental wellness and health.

Impact

MindReads aims to contribute towards improved mental health outcomes for individuals worldwide.

Working
